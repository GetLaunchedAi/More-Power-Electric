About More Power Electric

More Power Electric is a trusted, family-owned company dedicated to delivering safe, efficient, and dependable electrical services. We handle residential wiring, commercial installations, system upgrades, and electrical troubleshooting across the Midwest.

We take the complexity out of electrical projects, ensuring every job meets code, operates flawlessly, and is built to last. By working directly with each client, we provide timely service, quality workmanship, and customized solutions for every need.

I’m Kurt Mauer, founder of More Power Electric. My mission from day one has been to provide fair pricing, dependable work, and the peace of mind that comes from knowing your electrical systems are in expert hands.
